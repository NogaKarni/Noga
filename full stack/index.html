Itay Levi

Itay Levi (born January 25, 1988) is an Israeli Mediterranean music singer.
biography Itai Levy was born in the settlement of Gan Yavne and grew up in the Shaarim neighborhood of Rehovot in a family of Yemeni origin.
Levi ben Zakonum and he has two older brothers. When he was 16 years old, his father - an unfit father, died of lung cancer.
His mother suffered five strokes, which left her with a nursing disability ever since. He did his service in the IDF as a staff sergeant at the Parachute School.
Itay Levy now lives in Nes Ziona.
On December 25, 2023, he bought the football team Maccabi Shaareim from League A as a tribute to his father, a fan of the team who passed away.
